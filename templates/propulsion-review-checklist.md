# Propulsion Review Checklist

- [ ] Motion estimate is bounded by range and timing.
- [ ] Acceleration estimate is supportable.
- [ ] Platform motion is accounted for.
- [ ] Known airborne, orbital, atmospheric, and sensor causes are screened.
- [ ] Power behavior is addressed.
- [ ] Heat rejection is addressed.
- [ ] Control behavior is addressed.
- [ ] Multi-sensor evidence is calibrated.
- [ ] Independent reconstruction is possible.
- [ ] Speculative vocabulary is not used as a conclusion.

Boundary: field propulsion and zero-point language are speculative hypothesis vocabulary only.
