# Zero-Point Energy Boundary

Zero-point energy is a real quantum physics concept.

Usable zero-point propulsion is not publicly demonstrated.

Do not equate vacuum fluctuations with usable craft power.

Treat zero-point language as speculative hypothesis vocabulary only.

## Public-Safe Usage

Allowed:

- "A vacuum-energy / zero-point hypothesis would require extraordinary calibrated evidence."
- "This repository does not claim usable zero-point power."
- "Zero-point language is included only as a boundary category."

Not allowed:

- "This proves zero-point propulsion."
- "The craft used vacuum energy."
- "Free energy is demonstrated."
- "A public video confirms zero-point power."

## Evidence Requirement

Any claim of usable zero-point propulsion would require independent scientific review, reproducible measurements, power accounting, thermal accounting, force production evidence, and exclusion of known causes. This scaffold contains none of those claims.
